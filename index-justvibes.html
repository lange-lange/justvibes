Dear VCFA MFA GD students.

We like the idea of just hanging out. Maybe we can think of some fun things to spur conversation with a specific prompt?

We asked one of our typography students if they ever had used a modular or baseline grid before, and one student responded with: No, just vibes.

So what if we call it: ‘just vibes’. And each month, the next pair of students changes the format up, with the only rule being: as long as everyone can participate.

At the end of each format, you can self-nominate in pairs for upcoming months! The next two self-nominating students would come up with a new prompt — and a unique background GIF or image within the painting frame.
